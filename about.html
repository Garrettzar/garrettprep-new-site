Founder Story

Why GarrettPrep Exists

Garrett Zaremba created GarrettPrep after realizing that a lot of SAT prep feels too distant, too generic, and too hard for students to connect with.

The idea is simple: students often learn better from someone who recently sat where they are sitting, understands the modern SAT, and can explain the test in a clear, relatable way.

The GarrettPrep Difference

  • Local in-person SAT prep in Wayne, NJ
  • Recent high-scoring student tutors
  • Private and small-group options
  • Clear communication with families
  • Prep built around score, goal, test date, and schedule

Philosophy

Students Learn Better When Prep Feels Relatable

GarrettPrep is built around recent student tutors, simple explanations, and in-person accountability.

Not Big-Company Prep

The goal is not to feel like a generic online program. GarrettPrep is local, personal, and built around the student’s actual situation.

Not Random Cramming

Students need a plan before test day: what to review, when to meet, what to practice, and how to stay consistent.
