Founder Story

Why GarrettPrep Exists

Garrett Zaremba scored in the 97th percentile on the SAT, but his own prep experience showed him what was missing. After using online tutoring, he realized he would have learned more, stayed more engaged, and enjoyed the process more if prep had been in person and led by someone he could actually relate to. GarrettPrep was built from that idea: local SAT prep taught by recent students who understand the test because they just went through it.

Philosophy

GarrettPrep Philosophy

GarrettPrep is built on a simple idea: students learn better from people who recently sat where they’re sitting.

Recent Student Perspective Matters

GarrettPrep connects students with recent high-scoring tutors who understand the modern SAT, remember what it feels like to prepare for it, and can explain strategies in a way students actually relate to.
