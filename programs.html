Prep Options

Choose the Prep Path That Fits

Some students need one-on-one attention. Others do well in a focused small group. The free consultation helps GarrettPrep recommend the right fit.

1

Private SAT Coaching

For students who want a more personalized plan, stronger accountability, or a bigger score goal. Private coaching is best when a student needs focused help around their score, test date, weak areas, and schedule.

  • One-on-one attention
  • Personalized pacing
  • Ideal for bigger score goals
  • Built around the student’s current score and target score
  • In-person at Wayne Public Library
2

Small-Group SAT Prep

For students who want structure, accountability, and a lower-cost option while still getting a serious SAT prep plan. Groups are capped at 4 students and organized by test date, score level, and availability.

  • Capped at 4 students
  • Grouped by test date and score level
  • Lower-cost option
  • Peer accountability
  • In-person at Wayne Public Library

How Placement Works

The goal is to avoid guessing. GarrettPrep uses the consultation to understand the student first, then recommends the right prep option.

1. Submit the Consultation Form

Start by filling out the free consultation form.

2. Share Score, Goal, Date, and Schedule

GarrettPrep looks at where the student is starting, where they want to be, and when they plan to test.

3. Get a Prep Recommendation

GarrettPrep recommends private coaching or small-group prep based on student fit.

4. Schedule Sessions

Sessions are scheduled in person at Wayne Public Library.
